Getting Started

Just getting started can be the hardest part in any process. This site is largely aimed at compiling all of the resources currently out and placing them in one location. This is not an exhaustive list by any means, feel free to shoot an email/pull an issue on GitHub if you have any suggestions.

  • What to expect:

  • 1. Determine Eligibility
    • Types of Disability Claims
  • 2. Submit your Intent to File
  • 3. Gather Evidence
    • Examples: DD-214, Medical Records, Supporting Statements (Friends, Family, Co-Workers)
  • 4. File and Submit Your Claim
    • File Your claim online
    • File In Person at the VA
    • Get Help From an Accreditated Representative
  • 5. After you file your claim
    • Check the Status of Your Claim
    • Dispute and Appeals process
